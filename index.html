Copyright Holders

Individuals, companies, and content creators who hold copyrights have their own DMCA teams or representatives. They use the DMCA to protect their intellectual property rights by issuing takedown notices to service providers when they believe their copyrighted material is being used without permission.

Service Providers

Internet Service Providers (ISPs), web hosting companies, and other online service providers have designated DMCA agents or teams responsible for handling DMCA takedown notices and addressing copyright-related issues. They are required to have a designated agent registered with the U.S. Copyright Office.
